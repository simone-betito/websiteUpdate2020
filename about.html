About Simone

Simone Betito is a Data Visualization Developer at Girl Scouts and a Master of Science graduate in Data Visualization from Parsons School of Design (The New School). She also holds a Bachelor of Commerce from Ryerson University. She has a penchant for arts, design and is currently building her path in the growing field of Data Visualization and Infographics. She is passionate about the ability to combine her love for analyzing key trends in data with her partiality for creativity and visual elements. She's also a fervent advocate for women in STEM fields and continues to volunteer her time with organizations who champion this.

On the side, Simone also provides data visualization and analytics consulting for small businesses, non-profits, research reports and scholarly theses, technology firms, retail, journalists and small business design projects. Simone enjoys a good Baroque painting and has mixed feelings about pie charts.
